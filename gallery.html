Our Journey

THE EVOLUTION OF AGNIHOTRI AEROSPACE

Journey Timeline

Hopper

The Hopper represents our first step toward building a fully reusable launch ecosystem. By achieving stable vertical ascent and precision landing, we transformed vision into hardware.

Hopper Engine Testing

Systematic testing of the Hopper vehicle in controlled environments to validate flight performance and safety protocols.

Rocket Model

Our first physical model of a reusable rocket, built to test aerodynamic and structural properties under various flight conditions.

Testing Phase

The successful testing of our first rocket model marked a pivotal breakthrough, demonstrating our ability to translate engineering into flight-ready tech.

Avionics Integration

Integrating advanced avionics systems to enable autonomous flight control and real-time telemetry monitoring for our fleet.

Avionics Layer

Our avionics layer integrates flight control systems and sensor fusion to enable autonomous vertical operations.

Thruster Development

Developing high-efficiency thrusters for precise attitude control and propulsion in reusable launch vehicles.

Future Horizon

Future Rocket Model

Executing a full-scale sounding rocket flight test including testing of active descent control and precision guidance systems targeting submeter-level landing accuracy.

Hopper Development

Developing meter-level accuracy to demonstrate vertical takeoff and vertical landing, and to validate the RCS and VT-RCS systems.

Engineering the unknown with next-generation reusable launch systems.
